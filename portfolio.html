// 06
iOS Consumer

Divi — Bill Splitter

An iOS app that ends the awkward math at the end of a restaurant meal. Snap the receipt, assign each line item to whoever ordered it, and Divi sends a pre-filled Venmo charge to everyone at the table — under a minute.

  • GPT-4o vision parses every line item, quantity, tax, tip, and fees — including discounts as negative line items.
  • Tap-to-assign with unassigned-item highlighting; tax and tip distribute proportionally by subtotal share.
  • Venmo deep-link sends an itemized note ("Tuna Tataki $18, Rock Shrimp Tempura $22") so everyone sees what they're paying for.
  • Pro tier: bill history, saved groups, watermark removal — all behind StoreKit IAP.
